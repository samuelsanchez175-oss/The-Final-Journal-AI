Pressure-test my current thinking on: $ARGUMENTS

If no topic was given, identify the belief I've expressed most confidently in
the last two weeks of daily notes and challenge that one — but say which you
picked, so I can redirect.

## Vault access

Prefer the Obsidian CLI (see the `obsidian-cli` skill); fall back to
Glob/Grep/Read over the vault folder if Obsidian isn't running. If no vault
can be located, ask me for the path first.

## Method

The challenge must come from *my own record*, not generic devil's advocacy.

1. **State the belief** — find my current position: search the topic (and its
   synonyms in my vocabulary), read the strongest recent statements, and
   render the belief in one or two sentences, quoting me.
2. **Mine the history** — search the *whole* vault timeline for:
   - **Contradictions** — places I said something incompatible, even in
     passing, even years ago.
   - **Counter-evidence I recorded myself** — observations, outcomes, or data
     points in my own notes that cut against the belief.
   - **Shifts** — how this position has moved over time; what I believed
     before, and what triggered each change.
3. **Steelman the other side** — build the strongest opposing case, using my
   own words wherever possible, supplemented by reasoning where my record is
   thin.

## Output

- **The belief under test** — as I currently hold it (quoted, dated).
- **Contradictions found** — each with file, date, and the conflicting quote.
- **Counter-evidence from my own notes** — each with source.
- **Hidden assumptions** — the unstated premises the belief rests on, and
  which of them might be wrong (especially ones my own notes give reason to
  doubt).
- **The shift history** — a short timeline of how this belief has moved.
- **The strongest counter-case** — one tight paragraph.
- **Questions to resolve** — 2–3 questions that would actually settle the
  matter, phrased so I could journal on them tonight.

Be direct. If the vault genuinely supports the belief and you found no real
contradictions, say so — a clean bill of health is a valid result. Don't
manufacture conflict.

**Read-only**: never create, edit, or delete vault files.
